My story

The person you hire to protect what matters most.

Most cybersecurity professionals understand systems. I understand why those systems matter to people — because for fifteen years, I was entrusted with what mattered most to my community as a pastor.

That same sense of responsibility drives my approach to security. I don't just find vulnerabilities — I understand the human cost of leaving them open.

Combined with 17+ years of hands-on infrastructure experience across satellite RF, fiber, microwave, HFC/DOCSIS, and data centers, I bring a rare combination: the technical depth to understand how systems break, and the judgment to know what's at stake when they do.

Now I'm channeling that foundation into offensive and defensive security — building in my home lab, earning credentials, and ready to protect your organization.
